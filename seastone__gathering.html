The Seastone Family Gathering

The Sea Stone Family Gathering is first and foremost a fundraiser for The Chinook Nation. The people whose ancestral lands we gather on. Twenty percent of each adult registration is paid forward directly to the Chinook Nation Tribal office.

Secondarily, our annual Family Gathering is a container for our community to come together to learn and practice coastal focused skills, while nourishing our relationships with the sea and with each other.

If you're here for genuine family reunion at the beach vibes while cultivating your coastal skills and relationship to place, The Sea Stone Family Gathering welcomes you.

We are a private gathering commuity, open by invite only. The first step of registering for our family gathering, or any of our other camps, is becoming an enrolled member of the Coast & Crest Cahoots Club. Once enrolled, you'll receive invitations to register for our camps.

If you have any questions, please email Flow at theseastonecamp@gmail.com
