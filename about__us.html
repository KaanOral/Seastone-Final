About Us

A bit about your Sea Stone Hosts "We" are Kaan, Flow, and Logan. We are part time residents of the Long Beach Peninsula. For the passt years, we have been cultivating our relationship with these lands and waters, and honing our coastal skills. In 2024 we began inviting our extended kinfolk and community to come and gather with us at our coastal home. In August of 2024 we hosted a small and mighty group of folks for the Sea Stone Seeders Camp. During that camp, we collectively dreamt up the vision for the Sea Stone Family Gathering. It's been an honor to invite our friends, family, and extended community to join us here to gather, learn, and nourish our relationships with sea.

See you at the Sea!
